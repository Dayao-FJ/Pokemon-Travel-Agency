Essential Travel Tips

Budget Planning

Plan ¥5,000-15,000 per day including accommodation, transport, and Pokémon merchandise shopping.

Essential Apps

Download Pokémon GO, Google Translate, and Hyperdia for train schedules to enhance your journey.

Cultural Etiquette

Learn basic Japanese phrases and bowing customs. Pokémon Centers appreciate polite international visitors.

Shopping Strategy

Visit centers early for limited items. Many exclusive products sell out quickly, especially seasonal merchandise.
